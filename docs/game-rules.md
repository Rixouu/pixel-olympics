# Game rules

Pixel Olympics is a **party drinking game** layered on a random pixel-art race. The game calculates sip counts on the results screen — agree on house rules before you start.

**18+ · Drink responsibly · Water counts too**

## Setup

1. Add **2–6 players** in the lobby.
2. Each player picks a **name**, **colour**, and **racer sprite**.
3. Choose **scene** and **race length**.
4. Toggle **power-ups** on (chaos) or off (pure speed luck).
5. Tap **START THE RACE** and watch the live standings HUD.

There is no skill input during the race — outcomes are simulated. You’re betting on your racer, not controlling them.

## Standard sip rules (built into the game)

After the photo finish, the results screen assigns drinks:

### Winner (1st place)

- **Deals** sips equal to the **number of players**.
- Example: 6 players → winner picks who drinks **6 sips** total (split or assign as the table agrees).

### Everyone else

- Drinks sips equal to **finishing place**:
  - 2nd → 2 sips
  - 3rd → 3 sips
  - …and so on

### Last place

- Gets **one extra sip** on top of their place count.
- The UI labels this as a **chug** call-out — interpret as your group prefers (full drink vs extra sips).

### Slowpoke clause

- Any player who **never led the race** gets **+1 sip of shame** (shown in results when applicable).

## Power-ups (when enabled)

Power-ups are random pickups during the race. They affect the simulation only — not sip math directly — but the results screen may show optional party prompts (e.g. “power-up holders sip when it wears off”).

| Power-up | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Boost** | Short burst of speed |
| **Star** | Speed boost + immunity to traps/hits |
| **Banana** | Drop a trap; others spin when hit |
| **Lightning** | Slow racers ahead of you |
| **Bubble (shield)** | Block one hit, then breaks |

Turn power-ups **off** for a simpler “pure luck” race.

## Race length

| Mode | Feel |
|------|------|
| **Sprint** | Quick rounds, good for warm-up |
| **Classic** | Balanced default |
| **Marathon** | Longer race, more power-up chaos |

## House-rule variants

Use these if the standard rules feel too harsh or too mild:

### Soft mode

- Last place drinks **place only** (no extra chug).
- Slowpoke clause **off**.
- Winner deals **half** the player count (round up).

### Hard mode

- Last place **finishes their drink** (table consent required).
- Slowpoke clause **+2** instead of +1.
- Anyone who used a **banana** that hit someone else takes **1 sip** when the race ends.

### Teams

- Pair players and **sum** their placements; lowest combined score wins dealing rights.
- Or: winning team picks one loser from the other team for the deal.

### Non-alcoholic

- Replace sips with **fun penalties** (truth question, dare, push-ups, swap seats).
- Same placement math — only the “currency” changes.

## Between races

- **RACE AGAIN** — same lobby and settings, new random outcome.
- **EDIT PLAYERS & SETTINGS** — change names, colours, scene, power-ups.

## Disclaimer

This game is for entertainment among consenting adults. Pace yourselves, look out for each other, and never drink and drive.
